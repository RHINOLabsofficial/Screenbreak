What is ScreenBreak

Not an app.
A complete system.

ScreenBreak is a premium behavioral change platform built on the intersection of neuroscience, behavioral psychology, and precision habit engineering. It gives you the exact tools, knowledge, and structure that clinical researchers use — delivered as a coherent, intelligent system.

The Science Behind It

Built on what
actually works.

ScreenBreak synthesizes research from behavioral economics, clinical psychology, and neuroscience — translated into tools you can use today, not principles to think about tomorrow.

Variable Reward

The same mechanism that drives slot machine addiction powers every social feed. Understanding this isn't abstract — it changes how you relate to the pull.

Friction Engineering

Inserting deliberate friction between impulse and action gives your prefrontal cortex time to engage. This is the fastest structural change available to you.

Identity-Based Habits

Research consistently shows that behavioral change sustained by identity ("I am someone who...") is three times more durable than goal-based change ("I want to...").
